
# Wilson Bank & Trust Board Retreat - AI Training App

## Overview
Customizing the existing TBA Forum presentation app for a **1-hour virtual board retreat session** with Wilson Bank & Trust. The session focuses on practical AI demonstrations and hands-on board engagement.

---

## Session Structure (4 Tabs)

### Tab 1: Introduction (~5 min)
**"Welcome & SM Advisors"**
- Brief introduction to Cory Kronheim and SM Advisors
- Session objectives and agenda overview
- Set expectations for the interactive format

### Tab 2: AI in Action (~15 min)
**"AI Introduction & TPRM Agent"**
- Live demo of "Vicki" the TPRM agent (link to SharePoint site)
- Define key terms: What's an "agent" vs. a "copilot"?
- Show what AI can actually do in a banking context
- This practical demo-first approach engages the board before theory

### Tab 3: AI in Banking (~10 min)
**"What Banks Are Doing with AI"**
- Current AI trends in community banking
- 2-3 practical use cases relevant to Wilson Bank
- Brief discussion of risks and considerations
- *After this tab: Wilson management takes over for 5-10 minutes (not in app)*

### Tab 4: Board Workshop (~15 min)
**"Hands-On AI for Board Members"**
- Practical ChatGPT use cases anyone can try today
- Examples: personal productivity, research, analysis
- **Board Oversight Section:** Key questions directors should ask about AI
- Guidance on their role in AI governance and oversight
- Consumer-focused since board doesn't have Copilot access
